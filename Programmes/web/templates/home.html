Welcome to DbCAS

A unified gateway to the rapidly expanding landscape of alternative splicing resources, DbCAS brings diverse databases together in one accessible, searchable platform. Built on a systematic survey of the databases used in more than 4,000 mainstream alternative splicing studies over the past 10 years, DbCAS records and integrates these resources into a curated collection to help you find the most suitable one. Whether you are exploring isoform biology, mining long-read datasets, or surveying transcriptomic diversity, DbCAS is designed to help find the right resource.
